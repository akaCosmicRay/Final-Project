What We Offer

Services Descriptions
Full Application Security Assessment (Pentest) Are you about to release a new product or big update to production? Don't let a new critical vulnerability ruin your big moment. Let us take a look at your application. We will use a combination of manual testing, industry standard security tooling, and custom toolsets to assess your application top to bottom. This is a great option for maintaining compliance and customer security requirements as well!
Lightweight Automated Assessment Not looking for a full pentesting engagement? No problem! We can also perform an automated assessment using multiple industry standard scanning solutions. We will then manually review each finding and provide a report of any true vulnerabilities discovered. This is great for smaller releases, shorter timeframes, or smaller budgets. We understand! Some projects just aren't scoped to include large security initiatives.
Continuous Scan Reports Are you looking for something a little more frequent than a one time assessment? We can plug into your environment to run security scans on a regular basis and provide reports you can use to enhance your application security throughout that apps lifecycle. We can perform static code scans, container scans, dependency checks, and more! This option is great for small teams who may not have the bandwith or knowledge to stand up, own, and manage various security tools.
Consulting Services Did you receive a message from a bounty hunter about a new XSS vulnerability they discovered? Are you building out a new solution and want to make sure security is being baked in? Do you need help standing up a new secure SDLC process that meets industry standards? We can sit down and discuss what your appsec needs are and how we can help!
Static Website Development Are you a small business owner looking for a site that drives customers to your door? We can create clean, attractive websites that you could manage yourself without the need to brush up on your front end development skills. We use web hosts that are extremely affordable to suite your needs and we even perform SEO on your site to further increase engagement.
